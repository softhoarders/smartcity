Features

Everything parking should have been

Monitoring, marketplace, wallet, and fairness — not four different apps.

Spot protection

Cameras watch your assigned plate. Get push alerts when someone else parks in your bay, with timestamps and evidence on demand.

Find parking

Search any place on the map, filter by price and distance, and book instantly or on a schedule — with top picks recommended for you.

Credits wallet

Top up in lei, pay for bookings and subscriptions, withdraw to your bank when you're ready. Promo and referral codes included.

Fair appeals

Request camera photos, appeal with context, and get AI-assisted review before human admins step in when needed.

Smart pricing

Owners set floors and ceilings; Spotflow suggests or auto-adjusts hourly rates from demand, weather, and occupancy signals.

Operator dashboard

Live maps, device health, verification queues, CSV exports, and analytics for buildings and small parking operators.

How it works

From camera to checkout in three steps

  1. 1

    Claim & verify

    Register your plates with official documents. Admins or AI verify ownership before you get full trust.

  2. 2

    Monitor or list

    Link a bay to your plate, or list empty hours for rent with manual or automatic booking approval.

  3. 3

    Book & settle

    Drivers search the map, pay in Credits, and park with their plate authorized on the camera for the booking window.
