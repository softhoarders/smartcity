FAQ

Common questions

Is Spotflow government parking enforcement?

No. Spotflow is for property owners, tenants, and communities who want monitoring, rentals, and shared records — not municipal fines or police action.

How do cameras know a renter is allowed?

When a booking is active, the authorized plate temporarily switches to the renter's plate for that window. When the booking ends, your assigned plate returns automatically.

Why do I need documents to add a plate?

Trust matters. Uploading registration or city paperwork lets us verify the plate belongs to you before granting full account capabilities.

Can I try it without signing up?

Yes. Use the demo login (demo / demo123!), then enter verification code 456789 to explore sample cities, maps, and flows with synthetic data.
